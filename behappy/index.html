If today is a reset

Not sober today does not mean the day is over.

Start with safety, honesty, and support. This section is not treatment or crisis care, but it keeps practical recovery resources and motivation close when the day needs a reset.

First

Make the next hour safer.

Do not isolate if the day feels risky. Move away from access, drink water, eat if you can, and contact a trusted person or support line.

Best practice

Tell the truth without turning it into a verdict.

Write what happened, what led up to it, and what support is needed next. Keep the language factual and kind.

Support

Use real people and real resources.

Consider a meeting, sponsor, clinician, trusted friend, treatment resource, or crisis line if safety is uncertain.

Motivation

The next honest step still counts.

A reset can become information. You are not starting from nothing; you are starting from what the day taught you.
