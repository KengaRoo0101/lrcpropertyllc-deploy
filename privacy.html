Privacy Policy

LRC Property LLC builds websites, AI-supported tools, intake forms, and reports. This page explains the current privacy posture for public LRC pages and product flows.

Information you provide

LRC forms may collect contact details, selected product interest, notes, and consent choices. Product-specific tools may collect the details needed to generate an on-screen route, report, checklist, or intake preview.

Traffic and use audit

LRC records site traffic and tool use for admin review, security, troubleshooting, and product improvement. LRC may set first-party visitor and session ID cookies to connect repeat activity in the admin audit. Audit records may include those LRC-issued IDs, time, page path, method, status, host, referrer, IP address, approximate country or Cloudflare data when available, browser user agent, and whether the activity was a page view, asset request, API request, payment route, preview-gate event, or admin event. LRC does not store passwords, raw cookie headers, admin codes, or full request bodies in this traffic audit.

Use of information

Information may be used to respond to requests, prepare follow-up, operate the requested tool, improve team-facing workflows, and maintain business records. Optional research or improvement use should be based on consent or anonymized where appropriate.

Private uploads and sensitive details

Only upload or submit information you have the right to use. Do not submit passwords, full government identifiers, bank details, full tax identifiers, or unrelated sensitive records through public forms.

Ideas and confidentiality

Public forms are not a confidentiality agreement. Keep trade secrets, unreleased inventions, client records, and proprietary details out of public forms unless a written agreement says otherwise. Submitting an idea does not transfer ownership of that idea to LRC, and it does not transfer ownership of LRC websites, agents, workflows, templates, code, brand, or platform structure.

Agent safety

Levi and LRC agent flows are designed to guide, draft, summarize, and prepare handoffs. They should not bypass paywalls, submit forms, publish content, purchase anything, send messages, upload files, or change access without clear approval.

Third-party services

LRC sites may link to or use third-party services such as hosting providers, email providers, analytics tools, official government sites, or payment processors. Those services have separate policies you should review.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to admin@lrcpropertyllc.com.
