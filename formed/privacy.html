Privacy

Privacy Policy

Formed. is operated by LRC Property LLC. This page explains the current privacy posture for the early Formed. site and formation-readiness tools.

Intake previews

The formation-readiness preview processes answers to generate the on-screen path. If submission consent is checked, the review may be stored for LRC Property LLC admin follow-up. If consent is not checked, the preview stays in the browser session.

Official-record details

Formed. may collect private setup metadata such as legal business name, Articles of Organization status, EIN status, copyright and trademark planning notes, mission statement drafts, and internal setup notes. Do not submit full EINs, Social Security numbers, bank details, passwords, or full official document text through public-facing forms.

Contact messages

If you contact LRC Property LLC by email, the information you send may be used to respond, evaluate fit, prepare a proposal, or support a requested service.

No sale of private business details

LRC Property LLC does not sell private business intake details submitted for a Formed. review. Future analytics or research use should be anonymized or based on consent where appropriate.

Third-party services

External links, payment processors, official government websites, email providers, and other third-party tools are governed by their own policies.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to admin@lrcpropertyllc.com.
